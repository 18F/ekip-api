Get Vouchers For Your Students

As a 4th grade educator, you can download a learning activity and print paper vouchers for your students. These vouchers will give them free access for up to a full year to all federal lands and waters.

You are a 4th-grade educator if ...

You are a teacher of 4th graders at a US school or a US military school, or

You are responsible for the engagement of 4th graders as part of a youth serving organization. This includes religious group leaders, camp directors and leaders of home-schoolers or after-school programs.

We've created a fun and interactive learning activity to help you introduce topics surrounding national lands and waters to your 4th-grade students.

Then generate vouchers

Once your students have successfully completed the activity, you can award them with vouchers. Each student will receive their own individual voucher, with a unique code. Complete this short form to access the vouchers.
