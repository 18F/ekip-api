How does it work?

It's easy! Just follow the steps shown below and you'll be visiting the outdoors in no time.

1

As a 4th grader, use this website to get and print a voucher.

Educators can get vouchers for their students (and download a related learning activity).

2

Plan a trip (it's a good idea to involve your parents during this portion).

We provide resources for educators to plan classroom field trips.

3

Exchange your voucher for a wallet-sized pass at particpating locations. Everywhere else, you can use your voucher for free entry (federal lands and waters only).

Be sure to print your voucher before you go!

4

Enjoy your federal lands and waters. Leave only footsteps, take only pictures.

Share your pictures and stories using #EveryKidInAPark

And then, do it all again. Your pass is valid for up to one full year

A few things to know:

  • Each voucher has a unique bar code. This means you can't photocopy your voucher and give it to your friends. However, you should encourage them to come here and get their own voucher if they are in the fourth grade.
  • You will need to print your voucher and bring it with you. Electronic copies (on your phone or tablet, for example) are not accepted. At unstaffed locations, you'll need to leave the voucher on the dashboard of your car.
