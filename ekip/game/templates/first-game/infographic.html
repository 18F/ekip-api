How large are the federal lands and waters?

Federal lands cover somewhere between 635 and 640 million acres (484,000,000 football fields) That's 28% of the 2.27 billion acres of land in the United States.

Federal lands and waters exist for preservation, recreation and the development of natural resources.

There are a 117 national monuments. Each presents an interesting view into our history and culture.
