What are federal lands and waters?

Federal lands and waters are owned by the American people (that means you) and managed by federal government.

Federal lands and waters include:

  • National parks.
  • National forests.
  • National monuments.
A large number of federal lands and waters are open for recreational use. {% endblock %}
