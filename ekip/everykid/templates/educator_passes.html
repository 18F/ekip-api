Educators

As a 4th grade educator, you can download a learning activity, and print paper vouchers for your students. These vouchers will give them free access for their 4th grade year, and the following summer to federal lands and waters.

You are a 4th grade educator if:

  1. you are a teacher of 4th graders at a US school or a US military school.
  2. or you are responsible for the engagement of 4th graders as part of a youth serving organization. This includes religious group leaders, camp directors and leaders of home-schoolers or after-school programs.

Lesson plans and activities

We've created fun, standards based learning activities that you can use to introduce topics surrounding federal lands and waters to your 4th grader students.

  • Compare and contract the types of environments found in national parks, describe what a national park is, and find information using maps.
    See activity details.
  • Make pinwheels and use the power of the wind to do work.
    See activity details

Vouchers for your students

Once your students have successfully completed the activity, you can award them with vouchers. Each student will receive their own individual voucher.
